about me

Since I built my very first computer with my dad when I was 12, I've always been interested in computers. This passion for computers very quickly led me to discover programming. This path found me studying for a B.Sc in Computer Science. Along the way I found a side hobby in web technologies such as HTML and CSS. I soon realized that I had missed a fork in the road at some point and took time off to find the right program for my affinity of the more artistic side of programming.

Finding the right program to fit my love for design as well as the more technical aspects of computers was challenging but I found it at the Art Institute of California: Hollywood where I'm currently working on my B.Sc in Graphic/Web Design. Meshing my background in Computer Science and my zeal of design, I've discovered a very well balanced future for myself. I use these two halfs of my life to create aesthetically pleasing, functional and dynamic web projects.

In my spare time I like to nerd out to things like the adorable yet powerful Raspberry Pi, expand my cinematic horizons, and find other random little side projects I find for myself.
